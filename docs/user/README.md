## Getting Started

### What is OpportuneX?

OpportuneX is designed to democratize access to opportunities for students in smaller cities across India. Our platform uses artificial intelligence to:

- **Discover Opportunities**: Find hackathons, internships, and workshops from across the web
- **Smart Matching**: Get personalized recommendations based on your skills and interests
- **Preparation Guidance**: Receive AI-generated roadmaps to help you prepare for opportunities
- **Voice Search**: Search using natural language in English or Hindi
- **Real-time Alerts**: Get notified about new opportunities and approaching deadlines

### System Requirements

#### Web Browser
- **Chrome**: 90+ (Recommended)
- **Firefox**: 88+
- **Safari**: 14+
- **Edge**: 90+

#### Mobile Devices
- **Android**: 8.0+ with Chrome 90+
- **iOS**: 13+ with Safari 14+

#### Internet Connection
- **Minimum**: 1 Mbps (optimized for slower connections)
- **Recommended**: 5 Mbps for best experience

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit OpportuneX**: Go to [https://opportunex.com](https://opportunex.com)
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Located in the top-right corner
3. **Fill in Your Details**:
   - Email address
   - Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Full name
   - Phone number (optional but recommended for SMS alerts)

4. **Location Information**:
   - City and state
   - City tier (automatically detected)

5. **Academic Details**:
   - Institution name
   - Degree program
   - Current year of study
   - CGPA (optional)

6. **Verify Your Email**: Check your inbox and click the verification link

### Profile Completion

After creating your account, complete your profile for better recommendations:

#### Skills Section
- **Technical Skills**: Programming languages, frameworks, tools
- **Domain Expertise**: Areas of interest (AI/ML, Web Development, etc.)
- **Proficiency Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced

#### Preferences
- **Opportunity Types**: Hackathons, Internships, Workshops
- **Event Mode**: Online, Offline, Hybrid, or Any
- **Maximum Distance**: For offline events (in kilometers)
- **Notification Settings**: Email, SMS, Push notifications

## Smart Search

### Basic Search

1. **Navigate to Search**: Click the search bar on the homepage
2. **Enter Your Query**: Type what you're looking for in natural language
   - Examples:
     - "AI hackathons in Mumbai"

     - "Machine learning workshops this month"

3. **View Results**: Browse through personalized results

Use filters to narrow down your search:

#### By Skills
- Select from popular skills or add custom ones
- Multiple skills can be selected
- Results will match opportunities requiring those skills

#### By Organizer Type
- **Corporate**: Companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon

- **Government**: Government organizations and PSUs

#### By Event Mode
- **Online**: Virtual events you can attend from anywhere
- **Offline**: Physical events requiring travel
- **Hybrid**: Events with both online and offline components

- Distance-based filtering for offline events
- "Near me" option uses your profile location

#### By Opportunity Type
- **Hackathons**: Coding competitions and innovation challenges
- **Internships**: Work experience opportunities
- **Workshops**: Learning and skill development sessions

## AI Instructor

### What is AI Instructor?

The AI Instructor is your personal preparation coach that creates customized roadmaps to help you succeed in opportunities you're interested in.

### Generating a Roadmap

1. **Find an Opportunity**: Search and select an opportunity you want to apply for
2. **Click "Get Preparation Roadmap"**: Available on opportunity detail pages
3. **Set Your Target Date**: When you plan to apply or participate
4. **Choose Focus Areas**:
   - Technical Skills
   - Project Experience
   - Interview Preparation
   - Domain Knowledge

5. **Review Your Roadmap**: AI generates a personalized plan

### Roadmap Components

#### Phases
Each roadmap is divided into logical phases:
- **Foundation**: Basic concepts and prerequisites
- **Skill Building**: Core technical skills development
- **Practice**: Hands-on projects and exercises
- **Preparation**: Final preparation and mock tests

#### Tasks
Each phase contains specific tasks:
- **Learning Tasks**: Study materials and concepts
- **Practice Tasks**: Coding exercises and projects
- **Assessment Tasks**: Quizzes and self-evaluation
- **Project Tasks**: Build portfolio projects

#### Resources
For each task, you'll get:
- **Articles**: Relevant blog posts and tutorials
- **Videos**: YouTube videos and online courses
- **Courses**: Structured learning programs
- **Books**: Recommended reading materials
- **Practice Platforms**: Coding challenges and exercises

### Tracking Progress

#### Task Management
- **Mark as Complete**: Check off finished tasks
- **Add Notes**: Personal notes and insights
- **Set Reminders**: Get notified about upcoming tasks
- **Adjust Timeline**: Modify deadlines as needed

#### Progress Visualization
- **Progress Bar**: Overall completion percentage
- **Phase Status**: Current phase and next steps
- **Time Tracking**: Estimated vs. actual time spent
- **Milestone Celebrations**: Achievements and badges

### Customizing Your Roadmap

#### Skill Level Adjustment
- Update your proficiency level for better recommendations
- Skip beginner content if you're already advanced
- Add prerequisite phases if needed

#### Time Constraints
- Adjust roadmap duration based on available time
- Prioritize high-impact activities
- Get intensive vs. extended learning paths

#### Learning Preferences
- Choose preferred content types (videos, articles, hands-on)
- Select learning pace (fast-track vs. comprehensive)
- Include or exclude specific topics

## Notifications

### Types of Notifications

#### Opportunity Alerts
- **New Matches**: Opportunities matching your profile
- **Trending**: Popular opportunities in your field
- **Last Chance**: Approaching application deadlines
- **Similar Opportunities**: Based on your saved items

#### Roadmap Updates
- **Task Reminders**: Upcoming tasks and deadlines
- **Progress Milestones**: Completion achievements

- **Roadmap Adjustments**: AI-suggested improvements

#### System Notifications
- **Account Security**: Login alerts and security updates

### Notification Channels

#### Email Notifications
- **Immediate**: Real-time alerts for urgent items
- **Daily Digest**: Summary of the day's opportunities
- **Weekly Summary**: Comprehensive weekly roundup

#### SMS Notifications
- **Critical Alerts**: Deadline reminders and urgent updates

#### Push Notifications
- **Browser**: Desktop and mobile browser alerts

#### Notification Settings
1. **Go to Profile Settings**: Click your profile picture
2. **Select Notifications**: Choose notification preferences
3. **Configure Channels**: Enable/disable email, SMS, push
4. **Set Frequency**: Choose how often to receive updates
5. **Save Changes**: Apply your preferences

#### Unsubscribe Options
- **Individual Emails**: Unsubscribe link in each email
- **Category-wise**: Disable specific types of notifications
- **Complete Opt-out**: Stop all communications
- **Temporary Pause**: Snooze notifications for a period

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Can't log in to account
**Solutions**:
- Check email and password spelling
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact support if issue persists

#### Search Not Working
**Issue**: Search returns no results or errors
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection
- Try simpler search terms
- Clear search filters
- Refresh the page
- Try different browser

#### Voice Search Issues
**Issue**: Voice search not recognizing speech
**Solutions**:
- Check microphone permissions
- Ensure quiet environment
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Try switching languages
- Use text search as alternative

#### Slow Loading
**Issue**: Pages load slowly or timeout
**Solutions**:
- Check internet speed
- Close other browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try mobile app/PWA

#### Notification Problems
**Issue**: Not receiving notifications
**Solutions**:
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address
- Check spam/junk folder
- Enable browser notifications
- Update notification preferences

### Browser-Specific Issues

#### Chrome
- **Clear Data**: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
- **Reset Settings**: Settings > Advanced > Reset settings
- **Update Browser**: Help > About Google Chrome

#### Firefox
- **Refresh Firefox**: Help > Troubleshooting Information > Refresh
- **Clear Data**: Options > Privacy & Security > Clear Data
- **Safe Mode**: Help > Restart with Add-ons Disabled

#### Safari
- **Clear History**: Safari > Clear History
- **Reset Safari**: Safari > Reset Safari
- **Check Extensions**: Safari > Preferences > Extensions

### Mobile Issues

#### iOS Safari
- **Clear Cache**: Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data
- **Check Storage**: Settings > General > iPhone Storage
- **Update iOS**: Settings > General > Software Update

#### Android Chrome
- **Clear App Data**: Settings > Apps > Chrome > Storage > Clear Data
- **Check Permissions**: Settings > Apps > Chrome > Permissions
- **Update Chrome**: Play Store > Chrome > Update

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is OpportuneX free to use?**
A: Yes, OpportuneX is completely free for students. We believe in democratizing access to opportunities.

**Q: Do I need to create an account to search?**
A: You can browse opportunities without an account, but creating one enables personalized recommendations, saved searches, and AI roadmaps.

**Q: How often is the opportunity database updated?**
A: We update our database continuously throughout the day, with new opportunities added as soon as they're discovered.

**Q: Can I use OpportuneX if I'm not from a Tier 2 or Tier 3 city?**
A: Absolutely! While we focus on serving students from smaller cities, anyone can use the platform.

### Search and Discovery

**Q: Why am I not seeing relevant opportunities?**
A: Make sure your profile is complete with accurate skills and preferences. The AI learns from your profile to provide better recommendations.

**Q: Can I search for opportunities in multiple cities?**
A: Yes, you can search across multiple locations or set your preference to "Any location" for broader results.

**Q: How do I save opportunities for later?**
A: Click the heart icon on any opportunity card to save it to your favorites. Access saved opportunities from your profile.

**Q: Can I get notified about specific types of opportunities?**
A: Yes, set up custom alerts in your notification preferences to get notified about opportunities matching specific criteria.

### AI Instructor

**Q: How accurate are the AI-generated roadmaps?**
A: Our AI is trained on successful preparation strategies and continuously improves. However, treat roadmaps as guidance and adapt them to your learning style.

**Q: Can I modify the generated roadmap?**
A: Yes, you can customize timelines, skip tasks, add personal notes, and adjust the roadmap to fit your needs.

**Q: What if I'm already familiar with some topics in the roadmap?**
A: You can mark tasks as complete or skip entire phases if you're already proficient in those areas.

**Q: How long do roadmaps typically take to complete?**
A: This varies based on the opportunity and your current skill level. Roadmaps can range from a few days to several months.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Why is voice search not working on my device?**
A: Ensure your browser has microphone permissions and you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

**Q: Can I use OpportuneX offline?**
A: The PWA version allows limited offline access to previously viewed content and saved opportunities.

**Q: Is my personal data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

**Q: Why is the website slow on my mobile?**
A: We optimize for slower connections, but very slow networks may experience delays. Try using WiFi or enabling data saver mode.

### Account and Profile

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Profile Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is irreversible.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, update your email in Profile Settings. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via email.

**Q: Can I have multiple profiles?**
A: Each email address can have only one account. If you need to change your information, update your existing profile.

### Opportunities and Applications

**Q: Does OpportuneX help with applications?**
A: We provide preparation roadmaps and guidance, but applications are submitted directly to the organizing companies/institutions.

**Q: Are all opportunities legitimate?**
A: We verify sources and use fraud detection, but always research opportunities independently before applying.

**Q: Can I get help with interview preparation?**
A: Yes, our AI Instructor can generate interview preparation roadmaps with practice questions and tips.

**Q: What if an opportunity link is broken?**
A: Report broken links through the feedback option on the opportunity page. We'll investigate and update the information.
